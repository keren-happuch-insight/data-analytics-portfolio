Café Harmony:
Operational Audit

I conducted a deep-dive operational audit for Café Harmony using Excel. The goal was to solve a major mystery: Why was the shop losing money despite being busy every single day? The Discovery: My data analysis revealed a "Silent Profit Killer." We were over-ordering low-selling tea by 30% while constantly running out of sugar and muffin mix. We were literally turning customers away because we lacked basic ingredients. The Result: I designed a new inventory tracker and a "Morning Pair" bundling strategy. By aligning our stock with what customers actually buy (Young Adult favorites), I provided a roadmap to increase daily revenue and fix the 3.05 customer rating.

Retail Sales Analysis: Identifying High-Margin Growth Opportunities

I analyzed sales data for Choco De Luxe to identify growth opportunities in the luxury confectionary market. I focused on seasonal peaks and product performance to optimize inventory for high-demand periods.
